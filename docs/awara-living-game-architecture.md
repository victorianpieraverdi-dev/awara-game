# AWARA Living Game Architecture

## Core Vision

AWARA is not only a card collection game. It is a living evolutionary system where the player's real experience becomes light, light becomes structure, structure becomes a world, and the world eventually becomes a divine game board.

AWARA is also not only an application. It is a meditation, a sensory ritual, a bridge between digital play and real life. The game should gradually teach the player to recognize the same principles in the interface, in the body, in the home, in the city, in the planet, in the cosmos and in living cooperation with other beings.

The central progression is:

```text
Tigel / Daily Experience
→ Light
→ Soul of the Player
→ Earth of the Player
→ Cosmos of the Player
→ Divine Board Game
```

The player begins not as a commander of an external game world, but as a soul entering a field of initiation. The game gradually reveals deeper layers only when the player has accumulated enough light, attention, experience, and inner structure.

## Main Formula

```text
Attention → Experience → Awareness → Light → Card → Structure → World
```

A card is not a random prize. A card is crystallized experience.

The player lives a day, writes into the Tigel, the Oracle reads the experience, the game detects energies, agents, matrices, elements and distortions, then the system grants light and may crystallize one or more cards.

## Game as Meditation

The game should feel like entering a sacred living space, not like filling a form.

The core daily flow should be simple:

```text
Enter the game
→ feel the atmosphere of the day
→ write or speak into the Tigel
→ receive an Oracle reflection
→ receive sound, visual guidance, advice and rewards
→ see the soul / temple / cosmos change
→ carry the practice into real life
```

The interface must support a meditative state through:

- soundscapes;
- generated music of the day;
- visual atmosphere;
- animated light, elements and spheres;
- card reveal rituals;
- slow transitions between levels;
- embodied practices;
- daily advice;
- sacred timing and calendar resonance.

The game is a large festival of spirit in small daily form.

Breath is one of the main bridges of AWARA.

The player does not only think about the body, the home, the Earth and the cosmos. The player learns to breathe them.

The breath path begins with the body and gradually expands:

```text
→ room

```

The purpose is purification, integration and expansion of awareness.

The player breathes through:

- the body;
- every channel;
- every meridian / nadi;
- every energy;
- every chakra;
- the place of living;
- the territory;
- Earth, Water, Fire, Air and Ether;
- samsara;

At first the player breathes through the five senses and fragmented perception. Later the player returns attention into the central channel. From the central channel the player learns to breathe with the planet, with the galaxy, with the universe and finally as the luminous breath of Brahma.

This should become both:

- a meditation system;
- a gameplay progression system.

Breath practices should be short and safe at early levels. The game should not force intense techniques. It should guide attention, awareness and gentle embodiment.

## The Tigel

The Tigel is the heart of the daily loop.

The player may write freely:

```text
What did I live today?
What did I feel?
What did I understand?
Where did I act?
Where did I distort reality?
Where did light appear?
```

Or the player may write through four spheres:

```text
Feet: what happened in action, body, matter, routine, work?
Heart: what happened in emotion, love, fear, pain, devotion?
Head: what happened in thought, clarity, decision, perception?
Cooperation: what happened with other beings, service, boundaries, exchange?
```

The Oracle should return both a poetic answer and structured data.

## Unlock Logic

### Earth Unlock

Suggested condition:

```text
Soul light >= 1000
and at least 3 elements have meaningful development
and at least 7 Tigel entries exist
```

### Cosmos Unlock

Suggested condition:

```text
Soul light >= 5000
and Earth light >= 2500
and at least 7 Star Temple zones are active
and at least 3 cooperation events exist
```

### Divine Game Unlock

Suggested condition:

```text
Total light >= 15000
and Cosmos is active
and player has a meaningful card collection
and at least one cooperative field has been opened
```

## Svetcoin and Light Exchange

Svetcoin should represent exchangeable condensed light, not the same thing as inner light.

Inner light:

- soul growth;
- cannot be traded away;
- represents development.

Svetcoin:

- exchange medium;
- can be earned through verified contribution;
- can support services, offerings, card exchange, social actions and cooperative creation.
